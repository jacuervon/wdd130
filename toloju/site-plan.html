Services

Development and redesign of web pages.

At Toloju we understand the effort you have invested in your small or medium-sized business, which is why we not only create a website, but we will show your customers and potential customers how great your products or services are.

Using WordPress, we offer you a site with infinite customization possibilities and the simplicity that this framework offers. But if it's not enough, we can create something new using cutting-edge programming languages like Angular or React.

E-commerce creation.

If your business does not yet have a virtual store, we provide you with a virtual store development service using technologies such as woocomerce and Shopify that will allow you to achieve those sales you always dreamed of.

But if you already have an e-commerce, there is no problem since we have a team in charge of optimizing your sales funnel through updates to the user experience.

Administration of CMS web pages.

But if you already have a website, there is no problem since we have a team in charge of analyzing the user experience, thus optimizing the experience they have on your website, thus generating more profits in your business.

SEO

Having a website is only the first step to generating profits through digital channels, your site must be visible from the different search engines where your clients can find you more easily, SEO Strategies are in charge of doing this.

Community Manager

Having social networks is something simple, but managing them can become more complex, which is why we provide you with a person in charge of studying your company and its identity to generate and manage your content to show on social media; such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, WhatsApp, or other social networks, your full potential and benefits that your products or services have.

But we not only focus on the organic part of digital channels, but we also provide services to manage your ad campaigns or create them if you don't have them yet.
